Camera movement for the module Graphical Visualisation Technologies (BHT MIM 20 W23).

To move the camera clockwise (left) press the left arrow button.

To move the camera counterclockwise (right) press the right arrow button.

To increase the radius of the camera press "n".

To decrease the radius of the camera press "Shift" + "n".
